About Me

My name is K Bharath Kumar. I am a B.Tech student from Telangana and also a sheep seller. I come from a farming family, and I started selling sheep to support farmers and buyers with healthy livestock at fair prices.

As a student, I am learning computer science and electronics, but I also continue my family tradition of agriculture and livestock. My goal is to combine modern education with traditional farming to create better opportunities for rural communities.

Values I follow in my business:

  • Trust and honesty with customers
  • Healthy and well-cared sheep
  • Fair pricing for farmers and buyers
